Welcome to Foodel

Foodel is an app designed to help businesses and community groups organise home deliveries of food and other items. The app is designed to be as simple as possible to use and can be downloaded and run on your mac or PC. You can also use a trial version on our own server at the link below.

Foodel will divide a list of deliveries into multiple routes according to your requirements. Try Foodel

How to use Foodel

It's as easy as 1, 2, 3!

1. Drag and drop a list of deliveries onto the Foodel app

2. Wait while the app does its thing

3. Download the maps of your delivery routes

About Foodel

Foodel was developed during the 2020 COVID-19 lockdown to help local businesses and community groups. The application is based around an Evolutionary Algorithm which searches for efficient routes. Foodel is designed to be easy to use and doesn't require any specialised knowledge.

Foodel has been released as open source and the source code can be accessed on . GitHub.
