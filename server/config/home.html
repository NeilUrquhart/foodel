Welcome to Foodel

Foodel is an app designed to help businesses and community groups organise home deliveries of food and other items. You can submit a new task to Foodel using the link below.

Foodel will divide your list of deliveries into multiple routes according to your requirements.

How to use Foodel

It's as easy as 1, 2, 3!

1. Drag and drop your list of deliveries onto the Foodel app here

2. Wait while the app does its thing

3. View and download the maps of your delivery routes

About Foodel

Foodel was developed during the 2020 COVID-19 lockdown to help local businesses and community groups. The application is based around an Evolutionary Algorithm which searches for efficient routes. Foodel is designed to be easy to use and doesn't require any specialised knowledge.

Foodel has been released as open source and the source code can be accessed on . GitHub.
